## CRM status guide

- `New`: enquiry received, no action yet.
- `Contacted`: first response/call sent.
- `Follow-up`: waiting for patient/client reply.
- `Booked`: appointment or consultation booked.
- `Won`: became a paying patient/client.
- `Lost`: not moving forward.
- `Not fit`: outside service area or wrong request.
