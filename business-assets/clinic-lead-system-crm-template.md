# Clinic Lead System CRM Template

Use two separate tabs so inbound submissions are not mixed with researched prospects.

## Tab 1: Inbound Form Submissions

Recommended columns:

- Submitted At
- Status (`New`, `Contacted`, `Booked`, `Follow-up`, `Won`, `Lost`, `Not fit`)
- Source
- Name
- Email
- Phone
- Business / Clinic
- Website
- Country / City
- Service Interest
- Message
- Page URL
- Owner
- Next Follow-up Date
- Notes

## Tab 2: Researched Clinic Prospects

Recommended columns:

- Business Name
- Clinic Type
- City / Country
- Website
- Contact Email
- Contact Page
- Phone
- Problem Noticed
- Opportunity Angle
- Score 1-5
- Outreach Draft
- Status (`Research`, `Reviewed`, `Ready`, `Sent`, `Replied`, `Not fit`)
- Follow-up Date
- Notes

## Operating rules

- Do not send outreach until the page is live and Jibrin approves the batch.
- Keep outreach manual and specific; no spam blasts.
- Treat automated website findings as triage. Manually review the top prospects before sending.
- If a clinic looks polished, classify it as `Polished but optimizable` instead of discarding it.
