FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a new website?

Not always. If the foundation is good, a cleanup may be enough. If the site is outdated, confusing, or hard to use on mobile, a redesign may be better.

Can you work on my existing WordPress site?

Yes. I can improve existing WordPress sites, fix issues, or rebuild the important pages while keeping the same domain.

Who is this best for?

Local service businesses, restaurants, salons, agencies, consultants, and anyone whose website should help generate calls, bookings, or quote requests.

What should I send first?

Send your website link, business type, city, and what you want more of: calls, bookings, leads, sales, or trust.
