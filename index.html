The problem

A website can look “fine” and still fail as a business system.

Attention is not captured

Visitors read the page, but the next action is unclear, slow, hidden, or split across too many channels.

Enquiries are not tracked

New leads sit in inboxes, WhatsApp, forms, or missed calls without a shared status or next follow-up date.

Follow-up is inconsistent

Without a simple process, good prospects go cold even when the website technically “worked”.
